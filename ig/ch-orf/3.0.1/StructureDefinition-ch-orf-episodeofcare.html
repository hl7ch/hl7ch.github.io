Resource Profile: CH ORF Episode of Care

Official URL: http://fhir.ch/ig/ch-orf/StructureDefinition/ch-orf-episodeofcare Version: 3.0.1
Active as of 2025-05-22 Computable Name: ChOrfEpisodeOfCare

Copyright/Legal: CC0-1.0

Profile to document the antecedent episode of care e.g hospitalisation in case of care transfer between instituitons e.g. hospitals, rehab. clinics, retirement homes etc.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: CH ORF Episode of Care
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: EpisodeOfCare/ChOrfEpisodeOfCareSUfferer
