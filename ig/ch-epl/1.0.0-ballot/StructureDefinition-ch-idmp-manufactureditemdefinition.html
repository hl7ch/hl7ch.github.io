Resource Profile: CH IDMP ManufacturedItemDefinition

Official URL: http://fhir.ch/ig/ch-epl/StructureDefinition/ch-idmp-manufactureditemdefinition Version: 1.0.0-ballot
Active as of 2025-05-22 Computable Name: CHIDMPManufacturedItemDefinition

Copyright/Legal: CC0-1.0

Profile of the ManufacturedItemDefinition resource for representing the characteristics of a medicinal manufactured item as contained in a packaged medicinal product.

Usage:

  • Refer to this Resource Profile: CH IDMP AdministrableProductDefinition, IDMP Ingredient, IDMP PackagedProductDefinition and IDMP RegulatedAuthorization
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Budesonidum-2.3mg-Tablet, ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Estradiolum-Norethisteroni-Transdermalpatch, ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Nirmatrelvir-150mg, ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Pembrolizumab-100mg-Vial... Show 4 more, ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Ritonavirum-100mg, ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Solvent, ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Trientinum-150mg-Filmcoatedtablet and ManufacturedItemDefinition/MI-Trientinum-250mg-Capsule
