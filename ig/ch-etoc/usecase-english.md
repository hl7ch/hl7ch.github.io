### Transition of care letter

Players:

* Order placed by: [Dr O. Rderplacer](Practitioner-ORderplacer.md), general practitioner at the [group practice ‘Happy Doctors’](Organization-HappyDoctors.md).
* Recipient of the order: [Dr O. Rderfiller](Practitioner-ORderfiller.md), Chief Physician [Internal Medicine ‘Happy Hospital’](Organization-HappyHospital.md)
* Patient: [Mrs S. Ufferer](Patient-SUfferer.md); she has had a heart attack.

[Mrs S. Ufferer](Patient-SUfferer.md) is on holiday at Plfegeheim Bergblick. Dr O. Rderplacer is in charge of this nursing home. An acute heart attack makes an admission to the [Internal Medicine ‘Happy Hospital’](Organization-HappyHospital.md) necessary. [Dr O. Rderplacer](Practitioner-ORderplacer.md) writes a referral containing all the necessary information and sends it to [Dr O. Rderfiller](Practitioner-ORderfiller.md), Head Physician [Internal Medicine ‘Happy Hospital’](Organization-HappyHospital.md). She also sends a copy of the registration to the family doctor [Dr med S.Meier](Practitioner-SMeier.md).
