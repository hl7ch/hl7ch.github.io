CH EPR mHealth Patient Profile for CH:PIXm Mobile Patient Identity Feed

The patient demographics and identifier information which can be provided in the CH:PIXm Feed according to the EPR. If the patient is already registered in a community, the MPI-PID SHALL be provided as an identifier. The EPR-SPID as an identifier MAY be added. The birthname can be added with the ISO 21090 qualifier extension.
